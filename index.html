About Me

I am a childish, joyful, eccentric sea sponge who lives in a pineapple in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom. I am currently a fry cook at the Krusty Krab, a job in which I am exceptionally skilled at. I lack a lot of knowledge and am a constant annoyance to those around me (especially my co-worker and neighbor, Squidward Tentacles), but I am very good-natured.

I am kind of clueless, abnormal, immature, fun-loving, and hyperactive sponge-guy with a happy-go-lucky personality. I am an extremely determined person, and will often stop at nothing to accomplish a task. I am also somewhat dramatic to the people who are kind to me: for example, to the mailfish. People say I am over-confident. Sometimes, though, I can show a devious side when trying to get my boss Mr. Krabs and his arch nemesis Plankton to work together. They say I sometimes tend to make mountains out of molehills, such as when I lose my nametag.
