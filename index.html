About Me

I am a childish, joyful, eccentric sea sponge who lives in a pineapple in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom. I am currently a fry cook at the Krusty Krab, a job in which I am exceptionally skilled at. I lack a lot of knowledge and am a constant annoyance to those around me (especially my co-worker and neighbor, Squidward Tentacles), but I am very good-natured.
